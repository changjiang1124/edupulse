Student Level Management
What are Student Levels?

Student levels help you categorise students based on their skill level and experience. This system-wide classification helps teachers and staff better understand each student's abilities.

Where are student levels used?

  • Student Profiles: Each student can be assigned a level (e.g., Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert)
  • Student List Filtering: Filter and view students by their assigned level
  • Student Forms: Select appropriate level when creating or editing student records

Example Use Cases:

  • Art school: Beginner → Foundation → Intermediate → Advanced → Professional
  • Music academy: Grade 1 → Grade 2 → Grade 3 → Grade 4 → Grade 5
  • Language school: A1 → A2 → B1 → B2 → C1 → C2
