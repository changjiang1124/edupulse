# Teacher Clock-In & Timesheet User Manual

This guide explains how to use the Staff Clock In/Out system to record your attendance, view your work history, and export your timesheets.

## Quick Links
-   **Clock In/Out**: [/clock/](/clock/)
-   **Timesheet Overview**: [/timesheet/](/timesheet/)
-   **Attendance History**: [/attendance/teacher/history/](/attendance/teacher/history/)
-   **Export Timesheet**: [/timesheet/export/](/timesheet/export/)

## 1. Accessing the Clock System

1.  Log in to the **EduPulse** portal.
2.  Go to **[Clock In/Out](/clock/)**.
    *   Alternatively, click on your name in the top-right corner to open the user menu and select **Clock In/Out**.

> **Note:** If you are accessing this page for the first time, your browser will ask for permission to access your **Location**. Please click **Allow**, as the system requires GPS location verification for attendance.

## 2. Clocking In

1.  On the **[Staff Clock In/Out](/clock/)** page, check the "Current status" section.
2.  If you are currently clocked out, you will see a **Green "Clock In" button**.
3.  Click **Clock In**.
4.  The system will verify your location and record your start time.
5.  Once successful, your status will update to "You are currently clocked in."

## 3. Clocking Out

1.  When you have finished your work day or shift, return to the **[Staff Clock In/Out](/clock/)** page.
2.  You will see a **Red "Clock Out" button**.
3.  Click **Clock Out**.
4.  The system will record your end time and calculate the duration of your session.

### Troubleshooting Location Issues
If the system cannot detect your location:
-   Ensure GPS/Location Services are enabled on your device.
-   Click the **Refresh location** button on the clock page.
-   If issues persist, you may proceed to submit, but the system will flag the record as "Missing Location" for admin review.

## 4. Viewing Your Timesheet

To view your attendance history and calculated hours:

1.  From the **Clock In/Out** page, click the **Timesheet** button in the top-right corner.
    *   *Direct Link:* **[Timesheet Overview](/timesheet/)**
    *   *Alternative View:* **[My Attendance History](/attendance/teacher/history/)** (shows raw list of clock actions)
2.  The **Timesheet Overview** page displays:
    *   **Summary Cards**: Total Hours, Days Worked, and Completed Sessions for the selected period.
    *   **Records Table**: Detailed list of your work sessions, showing date, time, duration, and facility.

### Filtering Records
By default, the timesheet shows records for the current week.
-   Use the **Start Date** and **End Date** selectors at the top of the page.
-   Click **Filter** to view records for a specific period (e.g., last month).

## 5. Exporting Your Timesheet

You can export your timesheet data to an Excel file for your own records or for invoicing purposes.

1.  On the **[Timesheet](/timesheet/)** page, click the green **Export** button (top-right corner).
    *   *Direct Link:* **[Timesheet Export](/timesheet/export/)**
2.  You will be taken to the **Timesheet Export** page.
3.  Select the **Date Range** you wish to export (Start Date and End Date).
    *   *Tip: Use the "Last 30 Days" quick button for monthly reports.*
4.  Ensure **Export Format** is set to "Excel (.xlsx)".
5.  Click **Export Timesheet**.
6.  The file will automatically download to your device.
